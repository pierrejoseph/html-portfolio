The Best Movies According to Pierre


My top 3 movies of all time.

The Rock

From start to finish, the rock is action-packed. It never lets up, a true action hero movie.
The best part is seeing how the antagonist had a change of heart, but his second in command didnt
which led them to have a fallout and turn on each other.

Ex Machina

Ex Machina is ever so relevent presently given the stride AI has a made over the past two years.
The question is, what does it mean to be human. Are we in danger of AI becoming too self-aware?

Inside Llewyn Davis

For every artist, whether it been singer, actor, or dancer, for every artist that makes it in their
respective field, there are ten others that did not, and twenty that gave up. Among the ones that did not
give up, there are those whom that lead to their own demise.
